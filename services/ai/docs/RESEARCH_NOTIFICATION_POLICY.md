# Research Notification Policy

Tujuan: admin mendapat progress penting tanpa spam.

Notify admin:
• deep_research_started
• deep_research_plan_created
• deep_research_done
• deep_research_failed
• deep_research_needs_approval

Tidak notify default:
• deep_research_source_collected
• query kecil per sumber

Tool: `admin_notify_progress`.

Batasan: repeated abuse counter untuk denied belum persisten.
